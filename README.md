# 👋 Hi, we’re ByteBoon

**ByteBoon is an enterprise software company building AI systems for business operations.**

We are developing **BoonOS** — an operational AI layer that connects to business systems, models how work actually happens, recommends decisions, executes controlled actions, and learns from outcomes.

## What we build

ByteBoon builds software for companies that need more than dashboards.

Our systems are designed to help teams:

- Connect enterprise data and business systems
- Model operational reality through **BoonGraph**
- Turn events into decisions through **BoonThink**
- Execute governed workflows through **BoonAct**
- Track outcomes and feedback through **BoonSense**
- Operate with approvals, auditability, and human control
